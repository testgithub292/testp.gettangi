Risk Warning for Investors

If You Can’t Handle Risk, This Isn’t the Project for You.

While we believe in the tremendous potential of the Tangi Token and have made every effort to reduce risk, it is essential that potential investors understand that there are inherent risks in any investment, especially in the cryptocurrency and cannabis industries. Please read the following carefully before making any investment decisions:

Do Not Invest If:


  • You Do Not Understand the Risks Involved: Cryptocurrency investments, including tokens like Tangi, are highly speculative and volatile by nature. If you are not familiar with how cryptocurrency works or the risks associated with blockchain technology, it is important to do thorough research or consult with a financial advisor before investing. Tangi’s market, while full of potential, is still in the early stages, and investments in early-stage tokens can carry higher risks than more established assets.

  • You Are Unable to Accept the Risk of Regulatory Changes: The legal landscape surrounding both the cannabis and cryptocurrency industries is evolving, and changes in regulations could have an impact on the value and use of the token. While we have taken steps to ensure full compliance with existing regulations, there is always a possibility that future regulatory developments could affect our business model or token operations. If you are not comfortable with this uncertainty, investing may not be right for you.

  • You Are Looking for a Short-Term Investment: Tangi is a long-term project with a clear strategy for growth and adoption. We are committed to building a sustainable platform for the cannabis industry, but this will take time. Our goal is mass adoption of the Tangi token, but it may take several years before significant returns are realized. If you are looking for a short-term investment or are unable to hold your investment for an extended period, this project may not meet your needs.

  • You Cannot Afford to Lose Your Investment: As with any speculative investment, there is a risk that you could lose all or part of your investment. While we are working hard to mitigate risks and have achieved significant milestones, the token market remains volatile. If you cannot afford to take on this level of financial risk, it is crucial that you do not invest in the Tangi token.

  • You Do Not Believe in the Long-Term Vision of the Project: The Tangi token is an integral part of our larger vision for transforming the cannabis industry’s payment systems. This vision includes the mass adoption of the token by consumers and retailers alike. If you do not believe in the long-term potential of the cannabis industry, blockchain technology, or the unique business model we have developed, you should reconsider investing in this token.

  • You Are Seeking Guarantees of Returns: No investment can guarantee returns, especially in the highly volatile cryptocurrency and cannabis sectors. While we are confident in the value of our token and the strategic decisions we have made, we cannot and do not guarantee specific financial returns. If you are looking for a guaranteed investment or cannot accept the inherent risks, you should not invest.

  • You Are Uncomfortable with the Current Market Conditions: The cannabis and cryptocurrency markets are currently in dynamic and often unpredictable conditions. While we believe that the Tangi token is positioned for significant growth, market conditions can change quickly. If you are not comfortable with market fluctuations or are looking for a more stable investment, you should consider whether Tangi is the right fit for you.

  • You Are Looking for a 'Pump and Dump' Investment: Tangi is not designed to be a speculative ‘pump and dump’ investment. Our model focuses on long-term sustainability and growth. We are building a compliant, reliable payment solution for the cannabis industry, and the success of our token is linked directly to the success of the business and its real-world utility. If you are seeking quick profits from price volatility or do not align with our long-term strategy, we strongly advise against investing.

  • You Cannot Accept Flexibility in Timelines: As part of our strategy, we do not adhere to rigid roadmaps or timelines. While we are fully committed to delivering results quickly, we will not sacrifice the quality or long-term success of the project simply to meet an arbitrary deadline. Timelines are estimates, and we require flexibility on deliverables. We will push for speed, but we will not take hasty decisions or rush to meet deadlines that could jeopardize the project. Our focus is on delivering success to our investors in a reasonable and timely manner, rather than failure in a super-fast manner.

    • We Are Not a Typical Crypto Project

    If you're looking for a traditional, speculative, or hype-driven crypto investment, we need to be very clear: Tangi is not for you. We are not a meme coin, nor are we a pump-and-dump project. Our approach does not follow the typical patterns you’ve come to expect in the “wild west” of the crypto space. We do not offer empty promises, fake milestones, or unrealistic "to the moon" roadmaps. We do not run our business based on the typical fluff that many crypto projects use to attract quick, unqualified investments.


We’re building something that lasts. If you’re looking for a serious, credible, and realistic investment that will potentially provide you with a tangible, profitable outcome, you’re in the right place. We don’t make wild claims about skyrocketing prices or instant profits. We offer the truth: a legitimate opportunity to be part of the first compliant payment solution for the cannabis industry with a token that has real utility, real end users, and a clear path to profit.

This is not a get-rich-quick scheme. If you want transparency, professionalism, and a solid plan grounded in reality, then Tangi is the place for you. But if you are looking for empty promises, wild speculation, and the typical crypto nonsense, you may want to look elsewhere. We are the real deal, and we will not compromise on our integrity.


In Conclusion:


Investing in the Tangi token is an exciting opportunity, but it is essential that you fully understand the risks involved. We encourage you to do your own research, consult with a financial advisor, and make an informed decision based on your risk tolerance and financial goals. Tangi is not for everyone, and we want to ensure that only investors who are aligned with our vision and understand the risks involved choose to participate. Thank you for your interest in Tangi, and we appreciate your consideration.
