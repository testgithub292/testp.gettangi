Overview & Revenue Model


At Tangi, our big play isn’t just a token—it's a fully integrated, compliant payment system for an existing $33B all cash market. Our business model is simple yet powerful: we are replacing outdated, cash-only transactions with secure, legal, and compliant digital payment processing. This shift enables both retailers and consumers to transition seamlessly from a cash-based system to the digital payment world we now live in.

The need for this solution is clear—retailers and consumers alike are seeking an easier, more efficient way to conduct transactions. Retailers have been burdened with handling large amounts of cash, which is not only inconvenient but also exposes them to security risks. Consumers, on the other hand, have been forced to carry cash to make purchases, despite the fact that we live in a digital world where cashless transactions are the norm.

By replacing cash with digital payments, we remove these obstacles and offer both retailers and consumers the opportunity to engage in a seamless, secure, and efficient transaction process. By offering a solution to a massive industry-wide problem, we unlock the potential of vast revenue streams through transaction fees—from the $33 billion in sales already existing within the cannabis market.


This is a solution that everyone in the ecosystem needs, and the beauty of it lies in its simplicity: we are helping the cannabis industry enter the digital age, without relying on cash, and providing a transparent, compliant alternative. And with the market already there, we are positioned to generate revenue from day one by tapping into the existing transaction volume in this $33B industry.

We start by offering what retailers are most comfortable with—traditional payment methods. When consumers initially interact with our payment solutions, they will be presented with a familiar and trusted traditional payment method, such as a debit or credit card. This creates a sense of comfort and familiarity, making the initial transition from cash to digital payments seamless for them. Once consumers and retailers are using the service and are comfortable with it, we introduce the token payment option.

The token ecosystem is a finely tuned machine, driven by four key participants: the retailer, the consumer, the company (Tangi), and the investor. However, what’s truly powerful is that three of these participants—the retailer, the consumer, and the company—do all the work. The retailer enjoys reduced transaction fees, the consumer revels in rewards and incentives, and Tangi benefits from the higher profits generated by the token, which is the most profitable payment method on our platform.

But the investor? They simply sit back and watch as all the activity unfolds. The retailer is motivated by cost savings, the consumer is driven by rewards, and Tangi benefits from increasing network adoption—all working toward the same end goal: increasing the use of the token.

Meanwhile, the investor—having made their move early and secured their position—gets to enjoy the exponential growth in value, without lifting a finger. While the rest of the system is driving value through active participation, the investor profits passively, watching as the demand for the token swells and the value grows. It's a system that works because everyone else is incentivized to make it work—leaving the investor as the ultimate beneficiary of a thriving, self-sustaining ecosystem.

In essence, it puts the investor in the driver’s seat: others do the hard work, while they reap the financial rewards simply for having placed their bet at the right time. As you can see from below, everyone in the chain is highly motivated to drive token use.

For retailers, the token offers lower transaction fees compared to traditional payment methods, and it also keeps them fully compliant with banking regulations. Additionally, the token fosters a more loyal and engaged consumer base by offering valuable rewards, which directly translates to higher levels of customer satisfaction and retention for retailers. This increased consumer loyalty creates significant benefits for retailers, driving repeat business and a stronger connection with their customer base.

For consumers the token is the most rewarding payment option available with the rewards, discounts, and exclusive benefits it offers. Consumers will earn rewards for every purchase they make using the token, ranging from discounts on products to loyalty points that can be redeemed for free items, special deals, or even further rewards within the cannabis space.

We have strategically designed our solution so that consumers are not just passive participants but actively engaged in the process. From the moment they make their first transaction, we will capture their details (with their consent) and create a direct line of communication with them. This means that every time a consumer makes a purchase, we can communicate with them through SMS, email, or app notifications, offering them personalized rewards or telling them about new promotions, exclusive deals, or upcoming sales. This creates a deeper relationship between the brand and the consumer, one that is built on value and trust.

Additionally, by using the token, consumers become part of a loyalty program that rewards them for their continued use of the token. This loyalty program is specifically designed to make the token the preferred method of payment over traditional methods. Because the token is more rewarding, it will incentivize consumers to use it more frequently and, ultimately, make it the go-to option for making purchases. This direct communication channel allows us to constantly remind and encourage them of the benefits they can access by using the token, further reinforcing adoption.

The strategic importance of this communication is clear: We don’t just capture consumers for a one-time use. We engage with them consistently, turning them into loyal, long-term users of the token. With the combination of instant rewards and ongoing engagement, we create a frictionless and compelling ecosystem where the consumer naturally gravitates towards token use—driving adoption in a way that doesn’t require any hard selling, but instead, delivers value and benefits with every interaction.

Through this continuous, direct connection, we are able to build a robust, loyal customer base. Each consumer who uses the token is not just a transaction—they are part of a growing ecosystem where they have direct access to exclusive rewards and benefits. This ongoing relationship solidifies the token as the preferred choice for payments, ensuring its success as the primary payment method in the cannabis industry.

For the company, There are numerous factors why we want the token to be used widely across the cannabis industry Firstly, out of the multiple payment options we offer from our terminals, the token is the most profitable payment option for us, despite the fact that it is cheaper for retailers and more rewarding for consumers. This may seem counterintuitive at first, but it is an extremely rewarding business model for all the parties involved in the transaction. Our payment structure is designed to ensure we maximize our profits while minimizing costs for everyone else in the transaction chain.


But how does one make more money by charging less??


Let’s break this down. The following figures are for simple illustration purposes only but the model is accurate.

  • Our traditional payment options, such as debit or credit cards, come with higher transaction fees, let’s call it 10%, which is split between various parties involved, including the card networks, issuing banks, brokers and resellers. We end up with approximately 1.5% net.
  • On the other hand, when consumers use the token, the transaction fees are significantly lower—we charge the retailer, let’s say 3%, but the key difference is that we keep the entire 3% ourselves. This means, for every token transaction, the entire fee is retained by us rather than being shared with banks or third parties, meaning our net profits increase significantly. Our Profit Margins Double, while Retailers Costs Half.

So, even though the retailer is paying less in fees and the consumer earns free rewards for using the token, we actually increase our profit as a result of token adoption, which directly increases the company’s revenue and scalability.

While higher profit margins are certainly a key motivator for us, they are not the only reason we are focused on maximizing token adoption

  • Control of the ecosystem: By encouraging the widespread use of the token, we effectively take control of the entire payment ecosystem. Unlike traditional payment methods that rely on card networks and banks, using the token means we no longer depend on these external financial institutions or networks. We are no longer at the mercy of the banking system or limited by the existing financial infrastructure.
  • This gives us the freedom to operate independently—and more importantly, to grow faster. Since we don’t have to share transaction fees with third parties, we can reinvest those profits into scaling the business, expanding to more retailers, and continuing to innovate. It’s a self-sustaining model that grows the ecosystem organically.
  • Disengagement from the banking system: This is a critical part of our business strategy. The cannabis industry has long struggled with banking access, and while we’ve worked hard to establish a legal and compliant banking relationship, our token-based system allows us to disengage from traditional banking rails. In doing so, we eliminate many of the regulatory risks that come with relying on banks and card networks. By controlling the token ecosystem, we ensure that we are fully compliant and independent, with complete control over the entire transaction process.
  • Security and scalability: When we are in full control of the payment ecosystem, we can ensure that the entire process is secure, transparent, and scalable. We don’t have to rely on third parties, which can introduce vulnerabilities. We are the sole custodians of the system, making it easier to scale quickly without interference from external financial entities. This allows us to capture the market faster and with greater security, making the entire business model more robust and reliable.
  • A powerful competitive advantage: By building an infrastructure that doesn’t rely on traditional banks or financial institutions, we give ourselves a huge competitive advantage over any potential competitors. While others in the industry continue to struggle with banking access and regulatory compliance, we’ve already solved those problems and built a platform that is ready to scale, with a model that is more profitable and independent.

The token is the key to this independence—allowing us to maximize profits, disengage from the banking system, and scale faster than any other provider in the market. It’s a win-win situation for everyone in the chain—consumers get better rewards, retailers save on transaction fees, and we maintain complete control of the ecosystem, driving profitability and growth.

In short, we believe that the widespread adoption of the token is not only profitable but also critical for the long-term success and sustainability of the entire Tangi platform. By switching to token payments, we not only position ourselves as the dominant payment solution in the cannabis industry, but we also ensure that we control every aspect of the payment process, from the consumer experience to the transaction itself—allowing us to grow faster, scale higher, and ultimately provide more value to everyone involved.
