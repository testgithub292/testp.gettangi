Mitigating Risk Factors

Mitigating Risk and Strengthening Confidence in Tangi Token


While no investment is entirely without risk, we at Tangi have worked tirelessly over the years to mitigate as many risks as possible, positioning ourselves as one of the most stable and reliable options in this space. Here’s how we’ve reduced the risks associated with our token project:


1. Team Expertise and Proven Track Record


One of the key factors in reducing risk is the strength of the team behind Tangi. With years of experience working with major companies across various industries, our team has successfully navigated complex markets, including high-risk environments like the cannabis industry. We have proven our ability to develop, implement, and execute on big ideas, and we’ve built the right team to continue driving success. This extensive experience allows us to foresee and mitigate potential obstacles before they arise.


2. Lessons Learned from Round 1


The completion of Round 1 has been a crucial step in solidifying our credibility and proving that our model is feasible. Round 1 gave us an invaluable understanding of the market, investor concerns, and the exact steps needed to scale effectively. By working through the challenges with our first 2,500 investors, we’ve refined our approach and positioned ourselves to be even more successful with Round 2. The feedback we received was crucial in developing a roadmap that focuses on the long-term sustainability of the token, which significantly reduces risk for current and future investors.


3. First-to-Market Advantage with Bank Partnerships


One of the most significant risk-reducing factors we’ve achieved is the successful establishment of a banking relationship, an achievement that no other cannabis payment provider has managed to do. This relationship sets us apart from other projects in the space that have failed due to lack of compliance or inability to access banking services. By being the first to offer a compliant payment solution and having full banking backing, we ensure that our services are sustainable, legal, and scalable. This makes our business model less vulnerable to external regulatory changes, giving our investors a solid foundation on which to build their returns.


4. Fully Compliant and Transparent Operations


One of the largest risks in the crypto and cannabis industries has been the problem of compliance. By being transparent and strictly adhering to all regulatory requirements, we significantly reduce legal risks that often plague other projects. Our dual-payment system, which includes traditional card payments and token payments, provides an additional layer of flexibility for consumers and retailers while remaining fully compliant with all applicable regulations.


5. Token Adoption Strategy


We've strategically designed our platform to ensure that the token becomes the primary payment method for consumers and retailers, which directly contributes to the long-term sustainability of the project. By capturing consumers first with traditional payment options and seamlessly transitioning them to token payments, we ensure that a ready-made user base will exist when the token publicly lists. As token adoption grows, so too will its value, making it a secure and highly valuable asset for investors. This gradual adoption curve mitigates the risk of volatility often associated with early-stage tokens.


6. Real-World Utility and Market Demand


Tangi is not just another speculative crypto token—our token has real-world utility. It addresses the long-standing problem of cash-only transactions in the cannabis industry, solving the payment issues faced by retailers and consumers alike. With 8,000 retailers and 60 million potential users, the demand for our token is not driven by speculation, but by a genuine market need. By capturing even a small portion of this $33 billion market, we position ourselves for massive growth, directly benefiting investors.


7. Imminent Rollout and Market Expansion


The imminent rollout of our services, starting with the launch of our Cannabis Superstore, provides a clear path to market adoption. As we gain traction with retailers and consumers, the visibility of our token will increase exponentially, creating a robust foundation for a public listing. This imminent expansion means that we’re not relying on abstract concepts—we’re relying on real, market-driven adoption.

8. Strategic Partnerships and Ongoing Collaboration


We are also continuously building partnerships with key players in the industry. From retailers to influencers and industry veterans, we have a strong network that supports the growth of our project. The ongoing collaboration with forward-thinking banks and financial institutions further strengthens our position in the market, ensuring that we remain ahead of the competition.


Conclusion: A Strong, Risk-Reduced Path Forward


We recognize that risk is inherent in every investment, but through our experienced team, strategic decisions, and a focus on regulatory compliance, we have significantly reduced those risks for our investors. Tangi has carefully positioned itself for long-term growth, with a solid foundation and a clearly defined path toward success. As we continue to scale and make real-world progress, the risk of investing in Tangi has been minimized, and the potential rewards are vast. We invite you to take advantage of the opportunity to invest in a project that not only reduces risk but actively works to ensure success for all its stakeholders
