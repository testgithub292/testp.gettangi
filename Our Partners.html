Our Partners


GetTangiNow (Tangi Token) operates as part of a broader ecosystem of specialist service providers, including Walk Solutions, 8Pay, 8Tech and Get8Tech. These entities were established to offer operational flexibility and enhanced resilience in the highly regulated sectors we serve, including the legal cannabis industry.

While each entity serves a specific purpose, they all work in unison toward the same mission: delivering secure, compliant, and innovative payment and banking solutions for underserved markets.

This structure has been reviewed and is fully understood by our strategic partners, including our banking partner. The core banking relationship is held through Walk Solutions, a trusted and transparent partner in our ecosystem.

This framework allows us to:

  • Ensure uninterrupted service for retailers and consumers.
  • Isolate and mitigate potential risk exposure across markets.
  • Remain fully agile in evolving regulatory environments.

The entities are managed by the same leadership team and built to maintain operational continuity across verticals, especially in an industry where naming, licensing, and compliance challenges can differ drastically from one sector to the next.

Get8Tech is a forward-thinking technology company dedicated to redefining the future of payments. We are committed to delivering cutting-edge, modern solutions tailored to the needs of today’s ambitious vendors.

From the outset, we recognized the limitations of legacy payment systems—fragmented technologies built on outdated infrastructure. In response, we designed and built our financial technology platform entirely in-house, purposefully engineered to meet the demands of a rapidly evolving digital economy.

Today, Get8Tech empowers leading businesses worldwide with a comprehensive suite of payment services, including our low-cost Tangi payment solutions, data-driven enhancements, and integrated financial products—all unified in a single, seamless platform.

Powering the Future of Payments Walk Systems has long been a trusted provider of reliable payment processing solutions, offering a full suite of services including credit and debit card processing, ACH transactions, and eCheck payments.

Now, we're taking the next step in innovation with the introduction of our blockchain payment gateway—enabling businesses to seamlessly accept Tangi crypto tokens as payment. Whether online or in-store, Walk Systems gives you the flexibility and power to transact through one of the most advanced and secure payment platforms in the industry.

Experience the future of commerce with Walk Systems—where modern technology meets proven performance.
