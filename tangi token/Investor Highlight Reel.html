Why Tangi is the Smart Investment Choice


Welcome to the Tangi Investor Highlight Reel, where we’ve distilled all the key advantages and investor-centric factors that make Tangi the perfect opportunity for your portfolio. We’ve done the hard work, and now it’s time for you to benefit.


1. First-Mover Advantage in a $33B Market


Tangi is the first to crack the code of cannabis payments with a fully compliant, transparent, and cost-effective payment solution. With 60 million potential consumers and 8,000 retailers in need of our services, we’re entering a $33 billion market that is practically untapped by compliant providers. As an investor, you’re getting in at the ground level of a market poised for rapid growth. This is not speculation — it’s a real, growing industry with a massive, underserved customer base.



2. The Token: Core of the Solution


At the core of Tangi’s innovative solution is the token. While our payment system includes traditional bank-integrated payment methods, the Tangi token is where the real value lies. Our token offers the most rewarding, cost-effective option for consumers and retailers alike, and its adoption is central to our business model. Every decision we’ve made has been to ensure the token becomes the preferred payment method, driving its value and increasing its utility. When the token publicly lists, demand will already be there, driven by real-world usage and growing adoption — not just speculation.


3. Limited Token Supply: Demand and Supply


Tangi has a maximum supply of 250 million tokens; upon public listing, there will be a limited circulating supply of only 50 million tokens. You and your fellow presale investors will control the entire circulating supply initially, with demand coming from up to 60 million end users, fuelled by $33B in transactional volume. With the supply so limited, the demand for the token has the potential to drive significant value and returns for early investors. As the market captures even a small percentage of the cannabis industry, the token value will inevitably rise. The math speaks for itself — the more market share we capture, the more valuable your tokens become.


4. Secured Banking Relationship — The Key to Success


What makes Tangi unique — and what makes it so investable — is our secured banking relationship. This has been the roadblock for every other payment provider in the cannabis space. Banks have refused to service the cannabis industry, leaving retailers and consumers without reliable, compliant payment solutions. Tangi is different. We’ve secured a partnership with a forward-thinking bank, giving us the ability to operate legally and offer compliant payment services. No other provider has managed this, which gives us an unmatched competitive advantage and makes Tangi the first legitimate solution in the market.


5. Proven Adoption Model for Seamless Growth


Our approach to capturing consumers and retailers is designed for mass adoption. By offering free services and advertising initially through our Cannabis Superstore, we naturally attract retailers and their customers, sparking curiosity about how we can offer legal payment solutions. This organic approach to acquisition eliminates hard-selling and builds trust — the foundation of lasting relationships. As consumers and retailers transition from cash to token, we are setting the stage for widespread adoption and growing demand.


6. Seamless Transition to Token Payments


The token is the most rewarding and cost-effective payment option, providing discounts, loyalty points, and exclusive benefits for consumers. Retailers save on transaction fees while offering an engaging rewards program to their customers. Everyone in the transaction chain benefits, from consumers to retailers to Tangi itself. This creates an ecosystem where the token naturally becomes the go-to payment method.


7. Scaling with Speed — Positioned for Mass Adoption


We expect to capture a large portion of the market very quickly, which will drive token adoption, increase value, and make our token the primary payment method for cannabis purchases across the country. The speed of growth is already built into our strategy, and with no competition in the compliance space, our progress will be swift.


8. Transparent and Independent


Tangi is built on transparency and credibility. We’ve been featured in independent journalism from reputable sources and industry publications, and we’re supported by 2,500 existing investors who have been with us since Round 1. The project is not reliant on paid endorsements or celebrity backing. Our success comes from our legitimate, transparent, and scalable business model. We’re here to give you the facts, and they speak for themselves.


9. Real Utility and Market Dominance


The cannabis industry has long been underserved when it comes to compliant payment options. Tangi has solved this problem, and now, with the ability to offer both traditional payment methods and the token option, we are well-positioned to dominate the market. The demand is already here — consumers and retailers are eagerly waiting for a compliant solution that offers transparency, reliability, and affordability. By getting in now, you’re securing your stake in a market-leading project with real utility, massive potential for growth, and an established customer base that will drive token demand when it lists publicly.


10. The Exit Strategy — Maximizing ROI


When the token publicly lists, the demand will already be there. With up to 60 million potential end users and 8,000 retailers needing our services, you’re not investing in a speculative token with an unknown future. You will potentially be able to sell your tokens to a market that needs them — driven by real utility and not just speculation. With only 50 million tokens in circulation, the potential for massive returns on investment is clear.


Conclusion:


With first-mover advantage, a secure banking relationship, and a token that is at the core of a revolutionary payment solution, this is a project that ticks all the boxes. The market is ripe for disruption, and Tangi is already in the driver’s seat. By investing in Round 2, you are positioning yourself for tremendous growth and ROI. The future of cannabis payments is here, and it’s built on the token. Don’t miss out on your chance to be part of it.
