How Our Solution Works

Capturing the Consumer:


Seamless Onboarding to Token Transactions


Our strategy for consumer adoption is simple, organic, and highly effective. We use familiar, compliant payment options—like credit/debit cards and tap-to-pay—to capture consumers from their very first transaction.

From the moment a customer makes their first transaction using our payment system, we establish direct communication with them via their cell phone.

  • Automatic Opt-In: By simply making a purchase, they opt-in to receive updates on all things Tangi, dispensaries, and cannabis-related offers. This is a standard industry practice, ensuring they stay informed about exclusive rewards, discounts, and promotions. allowing us to keep them engaged with special offers, discounts, exclusive rewards, promotions and new product launches.
  • App Integration for Ongoing Engagement: Once they download our app, we take communication to the next level—offering personalized promotions, tracking rewards, and providing seamless access to our token benefits.

This direct line ensures that every customer remains engaged, making it effortless to guide them toward token payments and maximizing their savings while reinforcing their loyalty to the Tangi ecosystem. Shortly after their first transaction, we incentivise them to download our app.

  • Download the App & Wallet – We incentivize consumers to download our app and wallet by providing high-value redeemable vouchers for their favorite dispensary. The process is effortless—all they need to do is download the app and set up their secure digital wallet using our simple, step-by-step guide
  • Get Their First Tokens – Once they’ve redeemed and spent their first voucher, we gift them another high-value voucher—this time, the only requirement is to load their wallet with a small amount of tokens. To make this transition even smoother, we provide free bonus tokens and a discounted rate on their first token purchase.
  • Start Using Tokens – After spending the second voucher, they are given a third high-value redeemable voucher, which can only be redeemed by using the tokens in their wallet as part of their next purchase.

Throughout this process, the app tracks their dollar spend vs. token savings, constantly showing them how much they’ve been missing out on by not paying with tokens—lower costs, better discounts, more rewards, and loyalty perks.

By the time they’ve redeemed their vouchers, they’re seamlessly using token payments without any pressure or hard sell. The continuous cycle of savings, rewards, and loyalty benefits naturally keeps them paying with tokens—making it the obvious, most rewarding choice and Creating a Dependence on Token Payments:

  • Every time they use tokens, they unlock even greater benefits—deeper discounts, more rewards, and priority access to deals.
  • Auto-replenishment becomes the obvious next step. Their wallet automatically refills when it runs low, ensuring they never miss out on rewards or discounts.
  • Over time, they become reliant on the token because it consistently delivers more value than traditional payments.

From Closed Loop to The Public Exchange


Once token use is ingrained in their routine, switching them from our closed-loop system to the public exchange is effortless:

  • Nothing changes abruptly—their auto-purchase remains, but instead of buying from us, their tokens now come from the exchange.
  • They continue using tokens as normal, while a self-sustaining cycle of continuous token purchases takes hold.
  • Every new store we onboard brings in thousands of new repeat consumers, further driving demand for tokens.

The only question left is: Will you secure your place before mass adoption takes hold?
