Welcome to the Privacy Policy of GetTangiNow Corporation (“Tangi”, “we”, “our” or “us”).

We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users’ personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information across our website(s), applications, and services (collectively, the “Services”).

This policy applies to all subsidiaries, affiliates, and partners of GetTangiNow Corporation, globally.


1. What Is Personal Information?


"Personal Information" refers to data or opinions that identify, relate to, describe, or could reasonably be linked to an individual. This includes, but is not limited to: your name, contact details, location, wallet address, payment information, IP address, cookies, and device identifiers.

---------------------------------------

2. How We Collect Personal Information


We may collect your Personal Information through:

  • Account registration on any Tangi platform
  • Information provided during user support interactions

Where applicable, you confirm that any personal data relating to third parties you provide to us has been collected with proper consent and understanding.

---------------------------------------

3. Information We May Collect


Depending on how you interact with us, the types of data we may collect include:

  • Identity information (e.g., name, ID, government-issued numbers)
  • Contact information (e.g., email address, phone number)
  • Financial details (e.g., bank account, wallet address, card data)
  • Transaction history
  • Device data (e.g., browser, IP address)
  • Usage patterns (e.g., login data, referral source)
  • Cookies and tracking data

---------------------------------------

4. Use of Your Information


We use your information to:

  • Provide access to the Tangi platform and services
  • Process transactions, payments, and withdrawals
  • Communicate updates, service changes, and promotional material
  • Improve and optimize user experience

5. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR Specific)

We will never sell your personal information.

Your information may be stored or processed in jurisdictions outside your home country, including the United States, United Kingdom, or the European Union.
